Instructions

The Multimodal Insights tool combines speech and gaze tracking to provide a seamless interaction experience. Here's how you can use it:

  • Speak: The tool listens to your voice commands.
  • Gaze Tracking: Eye gaze is tracked to disambiguate speech inputs.
  • Granular Queries: Select a set of people, a data field, and a date range to make specific queries.
  • Best and Worst Analysis: You can ask the system who performed the best or worst and specify a time period. It will provide an analysis for the given date range or month. Use the keywords "best performance" or "worst performance"
  • Date Range Omission: If a date range is omitted, the system will provide results for the entire available time.
  • System Response: The system will respond to your queries based on the selected parameters with a voice answer and text answer printed to the terminal.

It is required to configure the gaze tracker for your computer. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Train Gaze Tracker" link below.
  2. Look at and click the targets until a red dot on the screen follows your gaze. At this point you can continue calibrating further or use the tool.
  3. The gaze tracker is sensitive to light changes, so you may wish to reconfigure. Click on the "Reset Calibration" link to do so.
